Does Nivalis Nights Have Photo Mode?

Confirmed photo-mode status and the details still awaiting release-build verification.

Quick answer: Yes. The official Nivalis Nights FAQ explicitly confirms a photo mode for virtual photography.

What is confirmed

Photo mode is part of the announced game scope. This is separate from Steam screenshots and means the game will provide a dedicated photography feature.

Controls are not yet published

The developer has not listed camera range, filters, depth of field, pose controls, time-of-day controls or whether NPCs and the interface can be hidden.

Release guide plan

The finished guide will document the activation shortcut, camera limits, available effects, output location and whether photo mode pauses schedules, weather or business operations.
