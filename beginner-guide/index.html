Nivalis Nights Beginner Guide

Use this spoiler-safe route for your first sessions in Nivalis Nights. Exact quest names should be filled in after launch, but the priorities are built around the game's life sim, business, exploration, and relationship loops.

First Week Priorities

1

Unlock repeatable income before buying cosmetic upgrades. Cash flow matters more than style in the opening hours.

2

Map transit, vendors, job boards, canals, and apartment services as soon as free movement opens.

3

Introduce yourself to named NPCs early so relationship and quest flags are not delayed.

4

Keep a small reserve for food, transit, bait, ingredients, or business stock.

Common Beginner Mistakes

  • Spending all early cash on decoration before establishing income.
  • Ignoring NPC schedules until a quest requires them.
  • Overlooking side activities like fishing that may bridge low-profit days.
  • Skipping official release notes that explain changed systems.
