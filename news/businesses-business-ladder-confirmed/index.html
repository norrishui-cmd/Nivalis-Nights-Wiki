Business Progression Runs From Small Stalls to Nightclubs

Official descriptions place food stalls, noodle stands and stim stores at the small end of the business ladder, followed by bars, restaurants and nightclubs.

Quick answer: The Q&A adds ingredients, upgrades, hiring, staff management and expansion into new districts as recurring parts of the management loop.

What the official update confirms

The Q&A adds ingredients, upgrades, hiring, staff management and expansion into new districts as recurring parts of the management loop. This wording comes from official Nivalis Nights store information, community Q&A or a dated ION LANDS development post. Why this matters for players

This confirms several venue types but not a single linear upgrade tree. Each may have different stock, hours, staffing and acquisition rules. That distinction helps players plan for launch without turning a promotional example into an unsupported rule, ranking or guaranteed outcome.

What we will verify next

Launch comparisons will measure setup cost, operating expenses, supply needs, management time and repeatable net profit. This page will be revised when a newer official announcement or reproducible release-build evidence changes the answer.
