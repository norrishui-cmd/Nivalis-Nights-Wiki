Cars Are Not Drivable, but Boats Are Player Controlled

The Q&A says players use automated HOVA taxis and trains between districts, cannot drive cars, and can pilot their own boat.

Quick answer: The boat opens freer exploration of water-connected parts of the city and supports expanding fishing locations and species over time.

What the official update confirms

The boat opens freer exploration of water-connected parts of the city and supports expanding fishing locations and species over time. Why this matters for players

This clear division prevents transport guides from promising vehicle customization or road driving that has not been announced. That distinction helps players plan for launch without turning a promotional example into an unsupported rule, ranking or guaranteed outcome.

What we will verify next

The release build must still establish boat acquisition, controls, docks, storage, upgrades, fares and exact transit routes. This page will be revised when a newer official announcement or reproducible release-build evidence changes the answer.
