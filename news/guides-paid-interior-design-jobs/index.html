Apartment Update Confirms Paid Interior Design Jobs

Players can decorate their own homes and also accept paid decorating work for clients encountered around the city.

Quick answer: Client jobs differ from free personal decorating because instructions, taste and specific furnishing requirements must be satisfied. Furniture comes from vendors with different assortments and material sets.

What the official update confirms

Client jobs differ from free personal decorating because instructions, taste and specific furnishing requirements must be satisfied. Furniture comes from vendors with different assortments and material sets. This wording comes from official Nivalis Nights store information, community Q&A or a dated ION LANDS development post. It is presented as a confirmed pre-release update rather than a finished-game walkthrough.

Why this matters for players

This creates a distinct guide category for briefs, required objects, budgets and completion triggers instead of treating every decorating page as a cosmetic gallery. That distinction helps players plan for launch without turning a promotional example into an unsupported rule, ranking or guaranteed outcome.

What we will verify next
