Nivalis Nights Fishing Uses Visible, Simulated Fish

The developers say fishing is based on physical fish in the water rather than a hidden random roll, with more than 1,000 fish supported in simulation.

Quick answer: A caught fish is the same entity the player saw before the catch. Fish form shoals, follow one another and can respond to bait, making observation relevant to future fishing guides.

What the official update confirms

Why this matters for players

Players should expect location and movement to matter, but no official species table, bait ranking or rarity formula has been published before launch. That distinction helps players plan for launch without turning a promotional example into an unsupported rule, ranking or guaranteed outcome.

What we will verify next

The launch guide will verify species names, water locations, conditions, bait responses, value and uses through repeatable catches. This page will be revised when a newer official announcement or reproducible release-build evidence changes the answer.
