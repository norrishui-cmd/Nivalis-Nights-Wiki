Greenhouses Can Supply Restaurants or Generate Sales

Players can rent multiple greenhouses, grow mushrooms, grains and vegetables, and either sell harvests or send them into restaurant supply.

Quick answer: Climate controls for temperature, humidity and light affect growth, while machines and advanced modules can automate processes such as irrigation.

What the official update confirms

Climate controls for temperature, humidity and light affect growth, while machines and advanced modules can automate processes such as irrigation. This wording comes from official Nivalis Nights store information, community Q&A or a dated ION LANDS development post. It is presented as a confirmed pre-release update rather than a finished-game walkthrough.

Why this matters for players

Greenhouses therefore create a make-versus-buy decision that includes rent, seeds, equipment, time, yield and the opportunity cost of manual management. That distinction helps players plan for launch without turning a promotional example into an unsupported rule, ranking or guaranteed outcome.

What we will verify next

Reliable profit advice must wait for final crop times, output, vendor prices, restaurant demand and automation costs. This page will be revised when a newer official announcement or reproducible release-build evidence changes the answer.
