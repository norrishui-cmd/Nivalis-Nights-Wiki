The Furniture Catalog Contains 620 Items

ION LANDS reports 620 furniture items and says persistent world systems remember more than 10,000 objects such as tables, chairs and dishes.

Quick answer: Furniture supports apartments, venues and paid client design jobs. Vendors carry distinct assortments, with sets differentiated by materials and atmosphere.
