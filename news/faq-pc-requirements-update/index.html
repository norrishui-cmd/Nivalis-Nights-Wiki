Steam Publishes Minimum and Recommended PC Requirements

The minimum lists 64-bit Windows 10 or 11, 16 GB RAM, 30 GB storage, DirectX 11 and a dedicated GPU with at least 6 GB VRAM.

Quick answer: The recommended tier keeps 16 GB RAM, raises VRAM to 8 GB, names RTX 2070 or newer as an example, and recommends DirectX 12 plus an SSD.

Why this matters for players

Neither tier promises a resolution or frame rate, so the requirements cannot yet support definitive 1080p, 1440p or 4K performance claims. That distinction helps players plan for launch without turning a promotional example into an unsupported rule, ranking or guaranteed outcome.
