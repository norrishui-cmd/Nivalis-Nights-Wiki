Optimization Update Reveals the Scale of Four Databases

ION LANDS says the game interface must handle more than 240 ingredients, 190 meals, 100 quests and 620 furniture items.

Quick answer: Those counts establish substantial database capacity and explain why the team has optimized more than 40 interface windows and panels for different display ratios.
