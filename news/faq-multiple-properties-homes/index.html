Players Can Own Multiple Venues, Apartments and Houses

ION LANDS confirms multiple restaurants or venues, apartments and standalone houses across districts, each with its own style.

Quick answer: The apartment preview adds that players may rent as many apartments as their wallet allows and that strategic locations can make movement more convenient.

What the official update confirms

The apartment preview adds that players may rent as many apartments as their wallet allows and that strategic locations can make movement more convenient. This wording comes from official Nivalis Nights store information, community Q&A or a dated ION LANDS development post. It is presented as a confirmed pre-release update rather than a finished-game walkthrough.

Why this matters for players

Property advice should compare cost, usable space, nearby transport, vendors and activities instead of selecting a best home from appearance alone. That distinction helps players plan for launch without turning a promotional example into an unsupported rule, ranking or guaranteed outcome.

What we will verify next

Rent rules, purchase conditions, fast travel, storage and property limits will be verified in the shipped game. This page will be revised when a newer official announcement or reproducible release-build evidence changes the answer.
