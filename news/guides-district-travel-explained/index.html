Developer Q&A Clarifies District Travel and Loading Screens

Nivalis uses large explorable districts, with HOVA taxis and trains moving players between them and loading screens during district changes.

Quick answer: Most business interiors can be explored within a district, while the player cannot drive cars. A personal boat provides direct control on water-connected routes.

What the official update confirms

Most business interiors can be explored within a district, while the player cannot drive cars. A personal boat provides direct control on water-connected routes. This wording comes from official Nivalis Nights store information, community Q&A or a dated ION LANDS development post. It is presented as a confirmed pre-release update rather than a finished-game walkthrough.

Why this matters for players

This gives guide writers a clear structure: district maps should explain local interiors and services, while transport guides should document transitions between areas. That distinction helps players plan for launch without turning a promotional example into an unsupported rule, ranking or guaranteed outcome.

What we will verify next

Exact stations, fares, routes and loading behavior still require the release build; pre-release footage is not enough for a final map. This page will be revised when a newer official announcement or reproducible release-build evidence changes the answer.
