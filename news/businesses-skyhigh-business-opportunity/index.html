Skyhigh Gardens Mixes Tourism With Business Expansion

The Skyhigh Gardens preview describes an exclusive elevated district with tourists, busy businesses, hanging gardens and a Japanese restaurant.

Quick answer: The developer suggests players may expand business there and observe competitors, while a flying taxi provides access to the district.
