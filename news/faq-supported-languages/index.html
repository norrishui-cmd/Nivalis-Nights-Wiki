Steam Lists 11 Supported Languages for Nivalis Nights

The current Steam page lists English plus French, German, Spanish, Japanese, Korean, Brazilian Portuguese, Russian, Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese and Turkish.

Quick answer: All 11 are marked for interface and subtitles, while only English is currently marked for full audio. The development team says localization QA covers the same language set.

What the official update confirms

Why this matters for players

Players should choose text language based on the storefront table and should not assume Japanese, German or other non-English full dubbing. That distinction helps players plan for launch without turning a promotional example into an unsupported rule, ranking or guaranteed outcome.

What we will verify next

Check Steam near release because language tables can change as localization and font testing conclude. This page will be revised when a newer official announcement or reproducible release-build evidence changes the answer.
