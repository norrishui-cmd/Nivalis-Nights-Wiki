Ghost System Keeps NPC Schedules Running Across Districts

The optimization update explains three simulation tiers for out-of-district, unseen nearby and visible NPCs, preserving schedules while reducing performance cost.

Quick answer: Characters outside the player’s district lose expensive visuals and physics but retain the information needed for their daily routines. Visible people are fully materialized.

Why this matters for players
