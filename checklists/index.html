Nivalis Nights Checklists

A checklist hub helps the site stay useful on launch day. Instead of thin pages with guessed data, this page organizes what must be verified and where each confirmed item should be published.

Launch verification checklist

Confirm release date, price, editions, PC specs, controller tags, Steam Deck status, business names, fish tables, NPC schedules, apartment upgrades, and known issues.

Player checklist

Wishlist the game, review specs, choose first business goals, track NPC introductions, and keep notes on time/weather effects.

Content update workflow

Update source-sensitive pages first, then add tested tables, screenshots, and spoiler warnings as real gameplay data becomes available.
