First Week Route

A practical launch-ready framework for new players. Replace placeholders with exact quest names after the final build lands.

Day 1 Priorities

  • 1Follow the tutorial until free movement opens, then note every vendor, job board, apartment vendor, and fast-travel point.
  • 2Spend only on income tools first. Cosmetic apartment buys can wait until a reliable daily route is paying out.
  • 3Talk to every named NPC once. Relationship systems in life sims often hide unlocks behind first-contact flags.
  • 4End the day with enough cash for food, transit, and one upgrade. A small reserve keeps the early game smooth.

FAQ

Short answers designed for search snippets and player confidence.

What is Nivalis Nights?

Nivalis Nights is a cyberpunk life sim set in a dense neon city, focused on earning a living, running businesses, fishing, decorating, exploring nightlife, and meeting characters.

Is Nivalis Nights the same as Nivalis?

The Steam listing uses the Nivalis Nights name, while older official materials may still refer to the game as Nivalis. This guide uses Nivalis Nights for current search clarity.

What should I do first?

Prioritize repeatable income, map knowledge, and NPC introductions before spending heavily on decoration. This keeps progression flexible once business costs begin to scale.

Will this guide include spoilers?

Yes, but major story, romance, and ending spoilers should live behind clear spoiler labels so beginner pages stay safe.
