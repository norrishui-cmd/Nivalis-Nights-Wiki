Nivalis Nights Quests

What is confirmed about Nivalis Nights quests, story progression, choices and the planned walkthrough database.

Quick answer: Nivalis Nights contains more than 100 quests according to the developer optimization update. Exact quest names and steps are not yet public, so this hub explains the verified scope without inventing walkthroughs.

How quests connect to the game

ION LANDS says major story beats link with gameplay systems and give rewards. The team has balanced quest difficulty, the economy and story pacing together, and says player choices are intended to feel meaningful. QA testing specifically covers quests and story progression.

Walkthrough quality standard

After release, each quest page will open with the objective and prerequisites, followed by exact steps, locations, decisions, rewards and known trigger problems. Choice consequences will use spoiler labels. A quest name alone, a generic summary or an unverified objective will not pass the publication gate.
