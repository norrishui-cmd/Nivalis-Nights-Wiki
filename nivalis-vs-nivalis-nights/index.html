Nivalis vs Nivalis Nights

Players may see both Nivalis and Nivalis Nights in search results, older official materials, and storefront references. This page exists to reduce confusion and make the guide easier to find whether someone searches the shorter original-style name or the newer Nivalis Nights wording. We use Nivalis Nights as the primary site name because it is the current guide brand and matches the user-facing domain, while still acknowledging that many official and historical references use Nivalis.

Why both names appear

The game has been discussed publicly as Nivalis, while the guide site and current search strategy use Nivalis Nights. Older pages, screenshots, articles, and store references can still surface for Nivalis searches, so both terms should be covered carefully in metadata and FAQ copy.

SEO handling

Use Nivalis Nights in titles and headings, mention Nivalis naturally in body copy, and avoid creating duplicate thin pages that only swap the name. This page is the canonical explanation for the naming overlap.

What not to assume

Do not claim a formal rebrand unless an official announcement confirms it. Treat the naming overlap as a search and source-context issue.
