Nivalis Nights Money Guide

This page is structured for tested profit data after launch. Until then, use it as a planning hub for business upgrades, jobs, fishing, and reinvestment decisions.

Early Economy Rule

Spend first on systems that generate more money: ingredients, tools, access, upgrades, or routes. Spend later on decoration once daily income is predictable.
