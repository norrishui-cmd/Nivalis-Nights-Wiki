Official materials point to a city with day-night rhythm and weather. For a life sim, those systems usually affect routing, availability, atmosphere, NPC movement, and activity timing. This page keeps confirmed planning advice separate from data that must be tested at launch.

Spoiler-Free Planning Advice

  • Group errands by district to avoid losing time to travel.
  • Do income tasks before optional exploration if the day has fixed deadlines.
  • Record NPC locations by time block during the first week.
  • Track weather alongside fishing, business traffic, and event availability.
